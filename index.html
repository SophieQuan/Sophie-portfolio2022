About Me

Hello! I'm Sophie Quan, a Web Designer and Developer. I have graduated Comtemporary Web Design at Durham College. I love creating simple, clean and accessible user interface designs to provide the best user experience.


In summer 2019, I had the opportunity to work with the City of Oshawa, along with my teammates, we designed and developed a platform that lets visitors use their smartphone to scan QR codes, navigate landmarks and points of interest at Oshawa Valley Botanical Gardens.


After graduated from the Contemporary Web Design program, I joined in an Internship as a Graphic Designer at Realtorshop.ca. I have designed and built templates for individual real estate agent’s branding. I also have the opportunity to create social media posts for sales associates to promote their businesses including Instagram, Facebook, Google Banner Advertisements, etc.


I’m currently opening for work, if you would like to hire me for your project, please feel free to contact me anytime.
